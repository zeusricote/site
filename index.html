About

Welcome to ZRLabs.

This is where I document my adventures in hardware hacking, cybersecurity, privacy, homelabs, locksport, and whatever other terrible idea captures my attention.

My approach is simple: understand how things work, build tools when I need them, and repurpose hardware whenever possible.

One day I might be building a Bus Pirate clone with a Pico. The next day I might be reverse engineering a router, reviving discarded electronics, or trying to make a mystery display do something its designers never intended.

I learn by taking things apart, asking questions, making mistakes, and documenting the process. If I break something, I'll show you exactly how I did it.
